What's xRO

xRO is a free-to-play RO server that seeks out to bring you the best and most fun Pre-Renewal experience possible. With us you can enjoy a multitude of official Instances, fully implemented 2rd jobs, functioning Achievement and Title Systems, and much more. We work hard on our updates and take pride in being able to offer something for everyone. So hop online and begin forging your own unforgettable adventure!
